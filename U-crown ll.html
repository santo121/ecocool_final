U-Crown II

Elegant tulip figure, two-stage compression, innovative air outlet, WiFi control, IFD dedusting technology.

Elegant flower appearance

Elegant tulip figure From the side, the panel and unit body look like a tulip in bud. When the unit is turned on, the panel slides upwards and the unit looks like a tulip in blossom. Rose-shaped swing louver The swing louver is designed in rose shape to fit the flower appearance. Pioneering seamless appearance The unit adopts seamless design to prevent dust. The panel adopts dual-color plastic injection and integrated molding.

Two-stage compressor for super cooling and heating in ambient temperature -30℃~54℃

Gree two-stage compressor can heat normally and cool normally under extreme temperature without increasing power consumption. Air outlet temperature can reach 50℃ in ambient temperature -20℃ and 13℃ in ambient temperature 50℃ respectively.

1Hz inverter technology; constant temperature operation; power input as low as 15W

Gree 1Hz inverter technology won China National Science & Technology Progress Award. Unit can achieve constant temperature operation, with power input as low as 15W. *Note: 15w means the minimum power for 9000btu, 12000btu and 18000btu models to operate stably at 1Hz under ideal condition.

Maximum air supply system

Pioneering air outlet design realizes 130° air supply and provides wider swing angle and farther air supply distance

WiFi control

DIY sleep curve: It can satisfy the sleeping requirement from various users. Just touch the screen slightly and then you can DIY your sleep curve.Regional air supply: After setting “room layout” in Gree special APP, you can set air supply area and control swing angle.DIY noise: You can set suitable noise value and then the unit will adjust operation automatically.Weekly timer: Set the weekly timer through mobile device. Air conditioner will be turned on or turned off automatically every day. From then on, you don’t need to worry that you will forget to turn off the air conditioner.

More Functions

IFD dedusting technology removes PM2.5 efficiently

3The unit not only adjusts air temperature, but also improves air quality. IFD dedusting technology removes PM2.5 efficiently to protect your health

Noise as low as 18dB

With optimized swing blade and relative position, the noise is lowered to 18dB. *Note: 18dB means the sound value when unit is operating in quiet fan speed.

Seven kinds of fan speed for your option

Seven kinds of fan speed: quiet, low, medium-low, medium, medium-high, high and turbo. You can choose the suitable fan speed as you like

Comfortable cooling
